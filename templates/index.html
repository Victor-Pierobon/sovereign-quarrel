Sovereign's Quarrel

Red's turn — select a piece
Pieces
Sentry — move 1-2 any direction. Protect it. Advance it to win.
Caster ▲ — move up to 5 hexes, can jump 1 enemy to capture. Cannot jump friendlies.
Striker ◆ — move exactly 3 in a straight line, jumps friendlies, captures 1 enemy along the way.
Shield ■ — move 1 hex orthogonally. Jump an adjacent enemy to capture it (must have empty hex beyond).
Win conditions
⚔ Normal win: Capture the opponent's Sentry.
★ True win: Move your Sentry into any of the opponent's Caster hexes (inner ring around their Sentry) and survive their next turn.
